Elegance. Simplicity. Usability.

The Three Ideals guide designs that are fun to use, visually attractive, and standards-compliant.

Elegance creates gorgeous designs that are pleasing to the eye and the mind.

Simplicity in design uses less instead of more, maximizing the impact of what remains. Simple designs are easy to use without sacrificing content or style.

Usability makes every interface adhere to the latest web standards, so that every user gets a memorable experience.

The Latest Technologies

Strong design ideologies are only half of a truly great creation. The tools of my craft are technologies such as PHP, MySQL, and Apache.
